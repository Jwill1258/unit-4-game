Rules of Conquest!

1. You will be given a random at the start of the game.
2. There are four crystals below, by clicking on one you will add a certain amount of points to your score.
3. You win the game by matching your total score to a random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.
4. The value of each crystal is hidden until you click on it.
5. Each time the game starts the value of the crystals and random number will change.
